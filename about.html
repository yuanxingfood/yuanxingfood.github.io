    Rongcheng Yuanxing Food Co.,Ltd is located in the featured cultivation area of Chinese Wakame & Kelp - Rongcheng, Shandong. Based on excellent quality and competitive price, the company has been processing and distributing products to tens of countries across the world since 2010.


    The product range mainly contains Frozen and Dried Wakame, Dried Aonori, Dried Hijiki, Dried Kelp and various of Japanese/Asian style Seasoned Foodstuffs. The factory is qualified by ISO 22000:2018 and HACCP systems.


    Our mission is to provide healthy, innovative and delicious gourmet foods to worldwide customers. We are open to talk about completely new products as we own both dried and frozen food processing lines.


    In 2018, we also started sales and marketing within China domestic market, and now we have two products (for OEM) frequently listed as Top-1 seller among Chinese E-commerce sites.


    Welcome to visit our factory and our booth of food expo. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any question.
